MITMUNC XV Committees

MITMUNC features many different committees that embark on a wide array of enthralling topics past, present, and future. MITMUNC XV committees will be released Fall 2022. Although our conference is in-person this year, we will hold 1-2 virtual committees to make our conference accessible to those unable to travel to MIT. Please refer to the following past committees as examples.
