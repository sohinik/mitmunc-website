MITMUNC XVII Logistics

Logistics, Locations, and Maps for MITMUNC XVII

Registration

Registration will be located in the Building 10 Memorial Lobby, right under the Great Dome. Please register your entire delegation at the MITMUNC booth, where you will receive folders, pens, and notepads. Enter via Lobby 7 at 77 Massachusetts Ave and proceed straight to the tables on the right.


Opening and Closing Ceremonies

The Opening and Closing Ceremonies will take place in Room 10-250, one floor above registration. Elevators are available for accessibility.


Committee and Advisor Rooms

All committee rooms and the advisor room will be in Building E51 (Tang Center), located at 70 Memorial Dr, Cambridge, MA 02142. Committee sessions are held on floors 0, 1, and 3.


Decorum and Dress Code

Delegates must adhere to professional, business attire (e.g., blazers, blouses, slacks, dress shoes). No harassment, bullying, or misconduct will be tolerated. Disqualification from participation or awards will result from violations of decorum.


Accessibility

MIT is wheelchair-accessible. Ramps to the Infinite Building require MIT tap access; contact us for assistance. Elevators are available in both the Infinite Building and E51.
